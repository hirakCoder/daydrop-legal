2. Countdown by Find Appiness — Best for Simplicity

Countdown by Find Appiness LLC is the most downloaded countdown app on the App Store with over 100K downloads per month. It's been around since 2018 and has a massive user base with 160K+ ratings.

Strengths

  • Simple, clean interface
  • 16 languages supported
  • iPad, Mac, and Vision Pro support
  • 160K+ ratings (4.8 stars)
  • One-time purchase model ($5.99 for widgets)

Limitations

  • No Dynamic Island / Live Activity support
  • No AI-generated backgrounds
  • Contains advertising in free version
  • Widget designs feel dated compared to newer apps
Verdict: Best for users who want a proven, simple countdown app. The massive user base and one-time pricing are appealing, but it lacks modern iOS features like Live Activities.

3. Countdown Star — Best Budget Option

Countdown Star has been a reliable countdown app for years. It focuses on core countdown functionality without trying to do too much.

  • Clean widget designs
  • Basic photo backgrounds
  • Affordable annual subscription ($4.99/year)
  • No Dynamic Island or AI features
Verdict: Good budget choice if you just need basic countdowns with widgets. Lacks premium features.

4. Big Day — Best for Weddings

Big Day originally started as a wedding countdown app and has expanded to general countdowns. It has a strong following in the wedding planning community.

  • Wedding-focused templates and themes
  • Basic Home Screen widgets
  • Simple, focused interface
  • One-time purchase ($2.99)
  • Limited widget variety
Verdict: Good if you specifically need a wedding countdown. For general use, DayDrop offers more features.

5. DreamDays — Best for Nostalgia

DreamDays has been around for over a decade and has a loyal user base. It supports both counting down to future events and counting up from past events.

  • Count up from past events (anniversaries)
  • Timeline view
  • Basic widgets
  • No modern iOS features (no Live Activities, no AI)
Verdict: Reliable for basic needs. Shows its age compared to 2026 alternatives.

6. Hurry — Best Minimalist Design

Hurry takes a minimalist approach to countdowns. Clean design, no clutter, just the essential countdown information.

  • Beautiful minimal design
  • One-time purchase ($1.99)
  • Limited widget options
  • Small feature set
Verdict: Perfect if you value minimal design above features. Not for power users.

7. Time Until — Best Free Option

Time Until offers a generous free tier with basic countdown functionality and simple widgets.

  • Good free tier
  • Basic Home Screen widgets
  • Clean interface
  • Limited customization
Verdict: Decent free option if you don't need premium features.

Which Countdown App Should You Choose?

If you want the most features: DayDrop is the clear winner with Dynamic Island, AI backgrounds, 10 widget placements, and 16 languages.

If you're planning a wedding: Big Day has wedding-specific templates, though DayDrop's AI can generate wedding-themed backgrounds too.

For most users in 2026, DayDrop offers the best combination of modern iOS features, beautiful design, and value. It's the only countdown app that fully leverages what iPhone can do — from Dynamic Island to Lock Screen widgets to AI-generated art.
