Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

DayDrop stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, track, or sell any personal information.

Data Storage

  • All countdown events, images, and preferences are stored locally in your device's app container using SwiftData.
  • Data is shared between the main app and widgets via an App Group container — this stays on-device.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party AI & Data Sharing

DayDrop uses third-party services to generate background images for your countdowns. Before any data is sent, the app asks for your explicit consent. You can decline and still use the app — local fallback images are always available.

Pexels API

When you use the curated background image feature, DayDrop sends a category keyword (e.g., "travel", "birthday") to the Pexels API to fetch a relevant stock photo. No personal data is sent. See Pexels Privacy Policy.

Together AI

When you use AI image generation, DayDrop sends the event title to Together AI to generate a background image. No other personal data is sent. See Together AI Privacy Policy.

Apple StoreKit

Premium subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple's App Store. DayDrop does not process or store payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Apple iCloud (CloudKit)

DayDrop syncs your countdown data across your devices using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is stored in your private iCloud account and is governed by Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy. DayDrop cannot access your iCloud data on our servers.

Notifications

DayDrop uses local notifications only to remind you about upcoming events. No push notification server is involved. Notification content never leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

DayDrop does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or advertising trackers.

Children's Privacy

DayDrop does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at daydrop@beatroot.dev.
