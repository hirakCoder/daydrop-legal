Common Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > DayDrop on your iPhone. You can cancel, change plans, or view your renewal date there.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select DayDrop from your purchase history.

How do I add widgets to my Home Screen?

Long-press on your Home Screen > tap the + button in the top left > search for "DayDrop" > choose a widget size and style > tap "Add Widget".

How do I add a Lock Screen widget?

Long-press on your Lock Screen > tap "Customize" > tap the Lock Screen > tap the widget area above or below the time > search for "DayDrop" > choose a widget.

My widget is showing the wrong event.

Long-press the widget > tap "Edit Widget" > select the specific event you want to display. If set to "Auto", it shows your pinned event (or the next upcoming one).

How does the badge countdown work?

DayDrop shows the number of days remaining on the app icon as a badge. It updates daily at midnight. The badge shows the count for your pinned event (or nearest upcoming event if none is pinned).

What's included in the free version?

Free users get unlimited events, Small & Medium widgets (Classic style), gradient and photo library backgrounds, day-of notifications, and up to 3 pinned events.

Does DayDrop work on Apple Watch?

Yes! DayDrop includes a full Apple Watch app with event list, detail views, quick-add, and watch face complications. The Watch app is a premium feature.
